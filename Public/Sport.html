From Beginner to Beast

  • 1. Strength Foundation

    • Build your core strength with simple yet powerful exercises.

    • Squats – 4 sets of 8 reps
    • Bench Press – 4 sets of 8 reps
    • Deadlifts – 3 sets of 6 reps

  • 2. Muscle Growth Phase

    • Sculpt your physique with targeted hypertrophy workouts.

    • Incline Dumbbell Press – 4x10
    • Lat Pulldown – 4x12
    • Bicep Curls – 3x12

  • 3. Lean & Strong Finish

    • Burn fat while keeping your hard-earned muscle.

    • Burpees – 3x15
    • Kettlebell Swings – 4x20
    • Mountain Climbers – 3x30s
